# Marketplace Platform Research — March 2026

## Gumroad Status: CONFIRMED — Instant Listing, No Approval

- No approval process, no store fees, no design requirements
- Account creation: minutes (email verify + payout method)
- First product live: under 20 minutes
- Payout: PayPal or direct bank transfer
- Fee: 10% flat on each sale (no monthly fee)

| Factor | Impact |

## Platform Alternatives Ranked by Speed-to-First-Sale

| Platform | Setup Time | Free Plan | Fee | Best For |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|----------|
| **Gumroad** | ~20 min | Yes (10% fee) | 10% flat | Fastest launch, largest creator marketplace |
| **Payhip** | ~30 min | Yes (all features, 5% fee) | 5% free / 0% on $99/mo plan | Best free plan, EU VAT handling |
| **Ko-fi** | ~15 min | Yes (0% on tips) | 0% tips, 5% shop | Tip jar + shop hybrid, community feel |
| **LemonSqueezy** | ~45 min | Yes | 5% + 50¢ | SaaS/subscriptions, tax compliance |
| **Sellfy** | ~30 min | No (starts $29/mo) | 0% transaction fee | Polished storefront, all-in-one |

### Recommendation
**Gumroad** remains the fastest path to listing. Payhip is the best alternative (lower fees, same features). Ko-fi is interesting for community-driven sales but less optimized for standalone digital products.
