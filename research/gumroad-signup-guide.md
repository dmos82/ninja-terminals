# Gumroad Seller Account — Signup Guide & Fee Reference

> Researched 2026-03-11 from gumroad.com/signup, gumroad.com/pricing, and Gumroad Help Center.
> DO NOT create the account — this doc maps every step so the user can do it quickly.

---

## PHASE 1: Account Creation (2 minutes)

**URL:** https://gumroad.com/signup

### Option A: Social Login (fastest)
Click one of:
- **Facebook** button
- **Google** button
- **X (Twitter)** button

This auto-creates your account using existing credentials. No extra fields.

### Option B: Email Signup
| Field       | Required | Notes                                           |
|-------------|----------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Email       | Yes      | Used for login + notifications + receipts        |
| Password    | Yes      | Has show/hide toggle                             |

Click **"Create account"** — by doing so you agree to Gumroad's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

**reCAPTCHA** — the signup form includes a reCAPTCHA challenge (auto or manual).

### Post-Signup: Email Verification
- Gumroad sends a verification email to the address you provided
- Click the link in the email to confirm your account
- You'll be redirected to the Gumroad dashboard

## PHASE 2: Account Settings (5 minutes)

**URL:** https://app.gumroad.com/settings (once logged in)

### User Details
| Setting           | Required | Notes                                                    |
|-------------------|----------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Email             | Yes      | Pre-filled from signup. Used for login + notifications   |
| Support Email     | No       | Shown on customer receipts. Defaults to your login email |
| Profile Name      | Yes      | Your store/brand name displayed publicly                 |
| Time Zone         | Yes      | Affects sales dashboard reporting                        |
| Currency          | Yes      | Default display currency (products charged in USD at exchange rate) |
| Notifications     | Optional | Toggle email/push for sales, free downloads, etc.        |

### Profile Setup
| Field             | Required | Notes                                                    |
|-------------------|----------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Profile Name      | Yes      | Set in the Profile tab of Settings                       |
| Profile Picture   | No       | Recommended — adds credibility to your store             |
| Bio               | No       | Short description of what you sell                       |

## PHASE 3: Payout Settings (10 minutes) — CRITICAL

**URL:** Settings > Payments

> You MUST complete payout settings before you can receive money. Gumroad will not let you skip this.

### Step 1: Choose Account Type
| Option       | When to Choose                                                           |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Individual** | Operating under your own name / sole proprietor / no business registration |
| **Business**   | You have business registration documents (LLC, Corp, etc.)              |

### Step 2: Identity Verification

**Requirements for ALL sellers:**
- Must be 18+ (or use legal guardian's details with permission)
- Must use full legal name (including middle name)
- Must use a physical address (NO PO boxes)

#### Path A: Bank Payouts (via Stripe)

Stripe handles credit card processing and bank payouts. They enforce KYC (Know Your Customer) and will request some or all of:

| Field / Document                    | Required | Notes                                                |
|-------------------------------------|----------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Full legal name                     | Yes      | Must match government ID exactly                     |
| Date of birth                       | Yes      | —                                                    |
| Phone number                        | Yes      | —                                                    |
| Physical address                    | Yes      | No PO boxes                                          |
| Government ID number                | Yes      | US: SSN or EIN. Other countries: national ID number  |
| Photo ID scan                       | Maybe    | Passport, driver's license, or national ID card      |
| Proof of home address               | Maybe    | Recent utility bill, bank statement, or rental agreement |
| Bank account details                | Yes      | Routing + account number (for direct deposit)        |
| Company Tax ID                      | If Business | US: EIN. See Stripe's list for other countries    |
| Business registration document      | If Business | —                                                |

**US-specific notes:**
- Tax ID = EIN (for businesses) or SSN (for individuals/sole proprietors)
- Name and DOB on SSN must match EXACTLY what you enter — check your physical Social Security card
- Middle name can be appended to the First Name field

**All data is encrypted and passed to Stripe. Gumroad's team never sees your documents or banking info.**

#### Path B: PayPal Payouts (simpler)

If bank payouts aren't available in your country:

| Field                  | Required | Notes                                    |
|------------------------|----------|------------------------------------------|
| Full legal name        | Yes      | —                                        |
| Physical address       | Yes      | No PO boxes                              |
| Phone number           | Yes      | —                                        |
| PayPal email address   | Yes      | Personal or business PayPal accepted     |

**No ID scan or documents required for PayPal payouts.**

> NOTE: You cannot switch from bank payouts to PayPal payouts (or vice versa) after setup.

## FEE STRUCTURE (as of March 2026)

### No Monthly Fee
Gumroad charges **$0/month**. You only pay when you make a sale.

### Transaction Fees

| Sales Channel                     | Gumroad Fee              | Notes                                    |
|-----------------------------------|--------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Direct sales** (your links, profile) | **10% + $0.50 per sale** | Customer buys via your link or profile page |
| **Discover marketplace**          | **30% per sale**         | Customer finds you through Gumroad's marketplace |

### Payment Processing (included)
- Credit card processing is handled by Stripe (included in the above fees)
- PayPal processing is available if you connect PayPal to checkout

### Tax Handling
- **Gumroad is a Merchant of Record** (since January 1, 2025)
- They handle ALL tax collection and remittance worldwide (VAT, GST, sales tax)
- You do NOT need to manage tax settings — it's automatic
- No action needed from sellers

### Payout Timing
| Method         | Processing Time    |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Direct deposit | 2–7 business days |
| PayPal         | 1–3 business days |

## WHAT THE USER NEEDS TO HAVE READY

### Minimum (for fastest signup):
1. Email address (or Google/Facebook/X account)
2. Password
3. A profile/store name
4. PayPal email address OR bank account details

### For full verification (will be requested eventually):
5. Full legal name (as it appears on government ID)
6. Date of birth
7. Phone number
8. Physical home address (no PO box)
9. Government ID number (SSN/EIN for US, national ID for others)
10. Photo of government-issued ID (passport, driver's license)
11. Business registration docs (if operating as a business)
