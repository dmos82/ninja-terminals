## 3. Zero-Audience Launch Playbook

### Pre-Launch (Do This Week)

1. **Create a free lead magnet** — A 5-page "AI Agent Orchestration Quick Start Checklist" on Gumroad (free, captures emails)
2. **Opt into Gumroad Discover** — Free marketplace traffic. 30% commission on Discover sales, but it's free distribution
3. **SEO-optimize your Gumroad page** — Use keywords: "AI agent orchestration", "multi-agent workflow", "AI automation blueprint"
4. **Start 15-25 value comments** in target subreddits before ANY self-promotion (Reddit will shadowban otherwise)

### Launch Day Sequence

1. **Reddit posts** — "I built an AI Agent Orchestration Blueprint — here's what I learned" in r/SideProject, r/SaaS, r/ChatGPT
2. **Twitter/X thread** — Break down the core insight. End with link. Tag relevant accounts.
3. **Product Hunt launch** — AI products dominate PH. Use 3+ images. Pre-collect followers before launch.
4. **Indie Hackers post** — Revenue transparency format ("Here's how I built and launched X in Y days")
5. **Discord communities** — Share value in CrewAI, AG2, n8n servers. Don't spam links.

### Post-Launch (Ongoing)

1. **Content-driven SEO** — Write 2-3 blog posts targeting "AI agent orchestration tutorial" and "multi-agent workflow guide"
2. **Email list** — Direct traffic converts at 5.8% vs ~2% for cold traffic
3. **Iterate based on feedback** — Start simple, let market tell you what it wants

### Critical Rules
- A simple 6-page checklist can outperform an 87-page comprehensive guide
- First product should solve ONE specific problem for a small group
- 15-25 helpful comments in a subreddit BEFORE any self-promotion
- Lead with value, not links — "Here's how to orchestrate 4 AI agents" with Gumroad link at the end

## 5. Competitive Positioning

### What Exists (Competitors)
- n8n workflow templates ($19-49) — visual, no-code focused
- Generic AI prompt packs ($5-19) — commoditized, price race to bottom
- AI automation playbooks ($29-49) — general automation, not agent-specific
- AI Agent Mastery course ($49) — video format, higher production cost

### Our Differentiation
- **"AI Agent Orchestration"** is a specific, emerging niche — less competition than "AI prompts" or "ChatGPT templates"
- **Blueprint format** — actionable architecture, not just theory or prompts
- **Multi-terminal/multi-agent** focus — this is what developers actually need now
- **Built with real experience** — we're literally running multi-agent orchestration (Ninja Terminals)

### Positioning Statement
> "The practical blueprint for developers who want to orchestrate multiple AI agents working in parallel — based on real production experience, not theory."
